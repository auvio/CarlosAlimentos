    • INGREDIENTS

    10 Oreo cookies (5 per person). 200 ml cow's milk, soy, almonds or whatever you prefer 1 cup of vanilla ice cream per person Whipped cream or Chantilly cream (optional)

    The Oreo frappé is so easy to make and with such a delicious result that, we warn you, you run the risk of becoming addicted once you try this recipe.



    Start by crushing the cookies but leaving some whole pieces as these will give flavor to this classic meringue. It is best to put the Oreo in a blender or use a blender or electric rod to get it

    Then add to the glass of the blender or the container where you are churning the cold milk and the vanilla ice cream. Start stirring with the stick or blender until all the ingredients are mixed obtaining as a result a thick shake, the typical texture of the frappé.

    Although you could serve your Oreo frappé right now, decorate it with a cookie and drink it whole, in one.As we want to recommend a much more professional preparation: serve this drink with whipped cream and chocolate syrup, as you will find in many restaurants and cafes.

    The whipped cream, whipped cream or Chantilly should go right over the frappé, decorating it and then serving to mix it with the drink and give it an even more sparkling and delicious touch. You can buy the cream already made
