    Fill the filter holder with grains.

    in a professional espresso machine or career, remove the filter holderr from the head. Fill it with freshly ground coffe, tap it lightly against your hand to distribute the grains and then the pressure to make them compact.[2]If you do not have access to a professional or homemade espresso machine, you can use an espresso kitchen. Pour the beans into the inner basket and spread them evenly with your finger.Use strong coffee instead of espresso if there is no way to prepare espresso.



    Prepare the espresso coffee.

    Return the filter holder to the position on the head and fasten it in place. Place a demitasse cup under the filter holder and let the water run to prepare the drink .Let the water run for about 30 seconds to extract the drink completely . Remove the espresso to distribute the cream, which is the foam that accumulates on the top. With a kitchen espresso machine, fill the tank with water at the highest level. Fill the filter inside the tank to the maximum level .Heat the espresso over medium heat until bubbles are formed on top. Pour the espresso into a cup demitasse.

    Heat the milk.

    Place the cold milk in a tall metal container. Hold the container at a 45 degree angle to the steam rod . Hold the container at a 45 degree angle to the steam rod. Heat the milk until it has increased in volume and the container is hot to the touch. Take out the container and clean the rod with a damp towel .[3] The ideal temperature for milk heated to steam is 60 ° C (140 ° F).



    Pour the milk and serve it hot.

    When the milk is ready, pour it into the espresso. Use a spoon to scoop a spoonful of the foam on top. [4] Serve the macchiato immediately. You can add sugar, decorate the top with cinnamon or take the macchiato as is.

    Place whipped cream on top

    Generally, macchiatos are served with whipped cream, but you can still decorate the drink with a little whipped cream, if you like. After adding any flavoring and pouring milk, place or sprinkle a spoonful of whipped cream on top of the drink.



    Decorate it with chocolate.Grated chocolate is delicious to decorate an espresso, especially if you already have some whipped cream on top. When the macchiato is ready, grate a chocolate bar directly into the milk or whipped cream. You can use bitter, milk or white chocolate to decorate the drink. Give it a little flavor with cinnamon.
