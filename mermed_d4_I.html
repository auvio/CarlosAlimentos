Coconut Jam

The coconut jam is a very natural jam.

Ingredients:

  • 500 gr. of dry coconut without sweetening.
  • 2 tablespoons of orange blossom water or roses.
  • 500 gr. of sugar.
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice.
  • 100 gr. of blanched almonds or 100 gr. of crushed pistachios.

Process:

1.- Sprinkle the coconut with orange blossom or rose water and with enough cold mineral water to make it moist, stirring with your hands. It is left to rest all night, so that it swells and softens.

2.- Prepare the syrup by boiling a couple of minutes the sugar with 150 ml. of water and lemon juice. The coconut is added and it is slowly brought to a boil, stirring constantly.

3.- Remove from the fire as soon as it starts to boil. Allow to cool a little before pouring it into cans.

4.- It is served in a bowl, sprinkled with almonds or crushed pistachios.

Number of Parts: 2 portions
Preparation: 25 Min
Cooking: 10 Min
