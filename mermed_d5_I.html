Jam of rose petals

The Petals of Rose jam is a very nutritious and natural jam.

Ingredients:

  • 250 g of red rose petals.
  • 250 g of sugar
  • One lemon's juice.
  • 3 tablespoons of water.
  • 3 tablespoons of rose water.

Process:

1.- Remove the roses, selecting the best petals and remove the white part of these, wash them.

2.- Dry the petals and soak them in the juice of a lemon for one hour.

3.- In a large pot, preferably copper, place the sugar, water and rose water; add the petals.

4.- Cook on low heat for half an hour, stirring constantly, until it reaches a boil; When it boils, it lowers the fire to a minimum and allows it to boil again at least three times.

5.- Once you have acquired candy texture, turn off the heat and let it rest for 20 minutes.

6.- Go to sterilized bottles and, if you are not going to consume it immediately, seal the vacuum.
