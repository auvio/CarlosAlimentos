Strawberry jam

Strawberry jam combines with bread and other desserts

Ingredients:

  • 1 kilo Strawberries, clean.
  • 4 cups of white sugar.
  • 1/4 Cup of Real Lemon Juice

Process:

1.- In a large bowl, crush and mash the strawberries little by little until you have 4 cups of crushed fruit. In a cast iron casserole, mix strawberries, sugar and lemon juice. Cook over low heat, continuing to move, until the sugar has dissolved. Raise the heat to high and let the mixture boil, continuing to move, until it reaches a temperature of 105 ° C (220 ° F).

2.- Pour into sterilized and hot jars, leaving between jam and the neck of the jar. Close well. Chill in a water bath if it is going to be consumed immediately otherwise, just refrigerate.

Total Time: 40 Min
Active Time: 20 Min
Preparation Time: 20 Min
Servings: 40
